課程大綱

The course emphasizes data structures and the development and analysis of their associated algorithms. Specific data structures include trees, graphs, stacks, queues, and hash tables.

授課老師: 林政宏(brucelin@ntnu.edu.tw)

課程內容

1. 演算法分析Algorithm Complexity

2. 陣列(array)

3. 堆疊與佇列(stack and queue)

4. 鏈結串列(Linked List)

5. 遞迴(Recursion)

6. 樹狀結構(Tree)

7. 二元搜尋樹(Binary search tree)

8. 堆積(Heap)

9. 高度平衡二元搜尋數(Height balanced binary search tree)

10. 圖形結構(graph)

11. 排序(Sorting)

12. 搜尋(Search)
